Our Lifeguards

Warren Young - Chief Lifeguard

Warren Young was employed by Merseyside Beach Patrol as a professional lifeguard in 1973 and was stationed at Formby Beach. In 1975 he was promoted to Chief Lifeguard and has now held the position for 41 years. Now one of the City's longest serving employees, Warren is responsible for facilitating beach safety along a 52 kilometre coastal strip.

Lauren Jones - Beach Patrol

Lauren Jones is a new employee at St Ives beach in Cornwall, however she has proved herself a worthy employee at the seaside, having rescued 3 people at the seaside since starting in December 2017. She has long term plans to work alongside senior staff and plans to become a Chief Lifeguard as soon as possible, and with her commitment we believe she'll make it in no time!
Michael Todd - Beach Patrol

Michael Todd has spent the past 4 years working at the beautiful coast of Prestatyn, Wales. He grew up with the dream of becoming a lifeguard and was trained up within months of finishing his education, he is now an established member of the team and thrives on helping people at the seaside.
